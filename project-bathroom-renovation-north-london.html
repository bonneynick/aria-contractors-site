The brief

A small, tired bathroom in need of a full update.

Our client's bathroom had dated tiling, an old electric shower and tired sanitaryware that no longer suited the property. With limited floor space to work with, the brief was to make the most of the room and bring it up to a modern, premium standard.

Our team carried out a full strip-out, re-tiled the room from floor to ceiling, and installed a new walk-in shower, wall-hung sink and toilet, and a heated towel rail, all carefully laid out to make the most of a compact space.
