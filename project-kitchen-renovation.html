The brief

From cramped galley to open-plan kitchen.

Our client's existing galley kitchen had reached the end of its life, with dated tile work, old cabinets, and a constrained layout dictated by a back door at the far end of the room. The brief was to open the space up, block up the redundant back door, and bring the room into the modern era.

Our team carried out a full strip-out, blocked up the original doorway to free up wall space for new cabinetry, installed a handleless high-gloss white kitchen with marble-effect worktops, and laid new engineered wood flooring throughout the room.
