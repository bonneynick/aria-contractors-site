How we work

Technology-led reinstatement.

Innovation and efficiency are at the heart of what we do. To run our insurance reinstatement claims, we built our own software, JSPER, so every job is managed with precision, transparency and control.

Built by a contractor

We didn't adapt to a tool. We built the tool.

JSPER was developed in-house from our own experience running reinstatement claims. It's the application for reinstatement contractors: a status-led process that runs the team, AI-powered scope parsing that handles any input format, and line-item production tracking that captures evidence as the work happens.

It means nothing falls through the cracks, and our insurer and loss-adjuster partners get full visibility from first notification to sign-off.

What to expect

Every step, handled with care

STEP 01

First Notification

The claim is logged in JSPER and the job enters a defined status. The right people are notified automatically from the outset.

STEP 02

Scope & Survey

Scopes from any format are parsed into structured line items in minutes, and our surveyor validates the works on site.

STEP 03

Planning & Allocation

Work is allocated by trade, scheduled and resourced. Every task is tied to a line item and an owner.

STEP 04

Reinstatement Works

Our skilled teams carry out the reinstatement, with progress and photo evidence captured against each line as the work happens.

STEP 05

Quality & Evidence

Status-led checks and full evidence trails keep insurers and loss adjusters informed throughout. No chasing required.

STEP 06

Completion & Sign-Off

A complete, evidenced record supports a clean handover and a fast, transparent claim settlement.
